# Vic's Quake 4 Mod: Momentum-Based Gameplay

This mod transforms Quake 4 into a fast-paced, roguelike-inspired shooter, drawing inspiration from the game *Mullet Madjack*. 
It introduces new gameplay mechanics and a new system to enhance the player's experience.

## How to Play:

- **Momentum System**: Start killing to fill up your momentum bar.
  - At 100% momentum, your ammo FULLY regenerates.
  - At 75% momentum, your health gauge is Maxed.
  - At 50% momentum, you are granted a double jump.
  - At 25% momentum, you gain a good chunk of movement speed.

- **Upgrades**:
  - **Persistent Upgrades**: Earn up to 5 basic upgrades to player/environment that last until death!
  - **Permanent Upgrades**: Unlock upgrades that persist through various runs! (armor gain + health upon death, etc...)

- **HUD Enhancements**:
  - A dynamic **momentum meter bar** is displayed at the bottom right of the default HUD
  - Default Quake 4 command line statements can be seen through the **in-game console** when momentum upgrades are granted

- **Weapon Modifications**:
  - Weapons have been rebalanced and re-themed to match the fast-paced gameplay style.

## New Features:

- Momentum-based gameplay mechanics
- Edited HUD with momentum meter
- Upgrade system with persistent and permanent upgrades
- Rebalanced and re-themed weapons
